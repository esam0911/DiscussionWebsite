Study Guide

Every week, the host will post a study guide here to aid you in your studies. The guide is not required, but is recommended as it will give you better understanding of the subject and will enable you to participate more actively in discussions.

    Individual Study Objectives

  • Find and study passages from the Bible which relate to the doctrine in question.
  • Come to a conclusion as to the meaning and truth of said doctrine.
  • Find as many extra-biblical sources (Sproul, Nicholeson, etc) on said doctrine as you want.
  • Fill out the weekly form which is attached below.
  • Be prepared to share your notes and research in the meeting.
